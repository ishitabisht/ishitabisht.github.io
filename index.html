Social Media

Storyboarding Sample

The EU really cares about the environment and is working hard to fight climate change. To show how they're teaming up with India on clean energy, my team and I created MarEU, a video inspired by Mario Super Bros. It was released on Earth Day and showed all the cool stuff the EU is doing to protect the environment. I wanted everyone, young and old, to have fun while learning about it!

Copywriting and Storyboarding Sample

The European Film Festival, an annual festival renowned for its captivating movies, encountered hurdles during the mid-year lockdown. Significance was given to expanding viewership and captivating a younger audience (18-35) to reignite the thrill. I spearheaded the creation of vibrant posts, gifs, and teasers showcasing prestigious awards from Cannes and TIFF. Managing social media postings and promotion budgets, I also curated compelling video scripts and teaser snippets with the video team.

Video Development Sample

Amid COVID-19 challenges, the EU and PLAN India joined forces for the International Day of the Girl Child, empowering a girl change-maker to take over social media accounts. I led the charge, crafting compelling video concepts, designing eye-catching templates, and penning impactful Instagram posts. The campaign shared real-life stories and engaging Q&A sessions, igniting awareness and support for girls' empowerment in the face of adversity.

Copywriting Sample

In the face of COVID-19 challenges, our campaign spotlighted McDonald's in southern India, emphasizing safety measures and employee growth. I led the charge, crafting engaging social media posts and selecting impactful visuals. Working closely with McDonald's team and my design crew, our goal was to showcase the brand's commitment to safety and employee development, creating a positive image in the region during tough times.
